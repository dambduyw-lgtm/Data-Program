To put this in perspective, this is an incremental $800 million above what we spent in 2023. At the same time, we intend to deploy those investments in a disciplined way. This quarter's results demonstrate how the scale of the business and strong expense discipline enable us to generate significant efficiencies, and those efficiencies are enabling us to invest at elevated levels while still generating significant leverage to drive strong earnings growth.

But we are confident in the ability of our business to support the year-over-year growth of around $800 million in marketing while delivering mid-teens EPS growth.
